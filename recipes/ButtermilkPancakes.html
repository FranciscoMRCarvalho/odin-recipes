Cheesecake Crepe Roll-Ups

Light, Fluffy, and Ready in 15 Minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 250 ml buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 1½ tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients – In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, vanilla sugar, baking powder, and salt
  2. Prepare the wet ingredients – Melt the butter and mix it with buttermilk and egg in a separate bowl.
  3. Make the batter – Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and mix until just combined. Do not overmix for fluffier pancakes
  4. Preheat the pan – Heat a pan on low to medium heat.
  5. Cook the pancakes – Pour spoonfuls of batter into the pan. Flip when bubbles form and cook until golden brown on both sides.
